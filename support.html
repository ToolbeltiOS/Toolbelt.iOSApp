Getting Started

First-Time Setup

When you first open Toolbelt, you'll be guided through creating your business profile. This information will appear on all your quotes and invoices:

  • Business name and contact information
  • Logo (optional, but recommended for a professional look)
  • Currency and tax rate
  • Default payment terms
  • Banking details for client payments

You can update these details anytime from Settings > Business Profile.

Creating Your First Invoice

  • Tap the + button on the home screen
  • Choose Quote or Invoice
  • Enter your client's information
  • Add line items with descriptions, quantities, and prices
  • Review the automatically calculated totals
  • Preview, then share or save as PDF

Quotes vs Invoices

Quotes are estimates you send to potential clients before work begins. They show your proposed pricing and help clients make decisions.

Invoices are payment requests sent after work is completed. They include payment terms and banking details for client payments.

Troubleshooting

My logo doesn't appear on invoices

Make sure you've uploaded a logo in Settings > Business Profile. Supported formats are PNG and JPEG. If the logo still doesn't appear, try re-uploading with a smaller file size (under 2MB recommended).

Calculations seem incorrect

Double-check your tax rate in Business Profile settings. The app automatically calculates tax based on your subtotal.

Voice input isn't working

Ensure you've granted microphone permissions to Toolbelt in your device Settings > Privacy > Microphone. Voice input also requires an internet connection to process speech.

I've reached my free monthly limit

Free users can create 3 documents per month. Your quota resets on the first day of each month. To create unlimited documents, upgrade to Pro in Settings > Subscription.
