Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our mobile application (the "App"), which allows contractors to create invoices and quotes in PDF format.

By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information as described in this policy.

2. Information We Collect

a. Information You Provide

The App may store information you enter manually, including:

  • Business name
  • Business address
  • Contact information
  • Client names and addresses
  • Invoice and quote details (items, prices, notes)

This information is entered by you and remains under your control.

b. Automatically Collected Information

We may collect limited, non-personal information such as:

  • Device type
  • Operating system version
  • App usage data (for performance and stability)

This data is used only to improve the App.

c. No Sensitive Data

The App does not collect:

  • Payment card information
  • Banking information
  • Government identification numbers
  • Location tracking data
  • Contacts from your device

3. How Your Information Is Used

Information is used solely to:

  • Generate invoices and quotes
  • Create and export PDF files
  • Improve app performance and reliability
  • Provide customer support if you contact us

4. Data Storage

  • All invoice and quote data is stored locally on your device
  • We do not upload your documents to our servers unless explicitly stated
  • You are responsible for backing up your data

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties.

Data may be shared only if required by law or to comply with legal obligations.

6. Third-Party Services

The App may use Apple-provided services (such as iCloud or analytics) subject to Apple's Privacy Policy.

7. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Your Rights

You may:

  • Edit or delete your data within the App
  • Stop using the App at any time

Because data is stored locally, deleting the App will remove stored information.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date.
